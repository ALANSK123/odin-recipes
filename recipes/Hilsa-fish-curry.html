Elish Macher Jhol

Description

This is hilsha-fish-carry one of the best bangla cousine

Ingredients

  • 6 pieces Hilsa fish
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 2 tbsp mustard seeds(soaked and blended)
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 3-4 green chilies (slit)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 cup warm water

Steps

  1. Marinate the Hilsa fish with salt and turmeric powder for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat mustard oil in a pan until it reaches a smoking point, then reduce the heat.
  3. Add the mustard paste, green chilies, and a little water, stirring well.
  4. Gently place the marinated fish pieces into the pan.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for about 10-12 minutes, turning the fish halfway through.
  6. Once the mustard sauce thickens, remove from heat.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
