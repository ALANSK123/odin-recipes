Steps

  1. Wash and soak chana dal for 30 minutes.
  2. Boil dal with turmeric, salt, and water until soft but not mushy.
  3. Prepare tempering by heating ghee/mustard oil and adding bay leaf, cumin seeds, cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves.
  4. Add ginger paste and saut√© for a minute.
  5. Mix boiled dal into the tempering and stir well.
  6. Add sugar (optional) and adjust seasoning as needed.
  7. Simmer for a few minutes until the dal thickens.
  8. Garnish with grated coconut and serve hot with luchi or rice.
