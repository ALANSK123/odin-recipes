Rosogolla

Description

Rosogolla is a soft, spongy Bengali sweet made from chhena and soaked in sugar syrup, loved for its melt-in-the-mouth texture

Ingredients

  • Chhena (paneer/cottage cheese)-> 1 cup
  • Sugar -> 2 cups
  • Water -> 4 cups
  • Cardamom pods (optional) -> 2-3
  • Lemon juice or vinegar -> 2 tbsp (to curdle milk)
  • Milk -> 1 liter

Steps

  1. Boil milk and add lemon juice/vinegar to curdle it.
  2. Strain chhena using a muslin cloth and rinse with cold water.
  3. Knead the chhena until smooth and form small, round balls.
  4. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling water and sugar, adding cardamom for flavor.
  5. Boil chhena balls in the syrup for 15-20 minutes until they double in size.
  6. Let them cool and soak in the syrup before serving.

Enjoy your Rosogolla
